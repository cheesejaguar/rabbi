# The Message Of The Year 5752: Spreading An Awareness Of G-d's Wonders

**Date:** September 8, 1991 (Elul 5751 / Tishrei 5752)
**Occasion:** The Rebbe's Message for the New Year 5752
**Source:** Sichos In English, Volume 50

---

The document presents the Rebbe's interpretation of the year 5752 through Hebrew letter acronyms. According to the text, these letters signify that "This will be a year imbued with wonders."

## Distinction from Previous Year

The previous year (5751) emphasized revelation of wonders through sight (Chochmah/wisdom). The current year focuses on comprehension and internalization (Binah/understanding), making wonders "lend themselves to being comprehended in their entirety."

## Primary Message

Communities should actively share awareness of divine wonders occurring around them. As stated: "we should instead endeavor to draw the attention of those around us to the G-dly nature of the wonders that transpire before our very eyes."

## Specific Phenomena Highlighted

The document emphasizes the mass aliyah (emigration) of Jews to Israel as an existing wonder, describing it as a preparatory stage toward the ultimate Redemption.

## Future Outlook

The text suggests we are "at the threshold of the Redemption" and that experiencing the imminence of redemption should inspire greater spiritual effort and joy.
