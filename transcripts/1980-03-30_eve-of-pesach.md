# 13th (Eve of the 14th) of Nissan, 5740

**Date:** March 30, 1980 (13th of Nissan, 5740)
**Occasion:** Eve of Pesach -- In the Synagogue After Ma'ariv
**Source:** Sichos In English, Volume 5

---

## Pesach Preparation and Spiritual Cleansing

The Rebbe emphasizes that careful observance regarding even minimal amounts of chametz (leaven) during Passover ensures spiritual purity throughout the year, as the AriZal teaches.

## National Security and Torah Adherence

He connects Israel's unstable security situation to inconsistency in following Torah law, specifically referencing Halachic rulings about defending settlements. "There is no strength other than Torah" serves as his foundational principle.

## The Role of Children

A significant portion focuses on how Jewish youth can influence their parents toward greater religious observance, drawing parallels to the Exodus narrative where children recognized divine intervention first.

## Historical Significance of the Date

The date is marked as the yahrtzeit (death anniversary) of the Tzemach Tzedek and Rav Yosef Karo, and the birthday of the Rambam -- figures representing different dimensions of Jewish jurisprudence and mysticism.

## Messianic Themes

The discourse concludes with aspirational language about universal Torah knowledge and the coming redemption, referencing the Rambam's vision that "the earth will be filled with the knowledge of G-d."
