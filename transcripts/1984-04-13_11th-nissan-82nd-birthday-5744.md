# 11th Day of Nissan, 5744 -- 82nd Birthday of the Lubavitcher Rebbe

**Date:** April 13, 1984 (11th Nissan 5744)
**Occasion:** 82nd Birthday of the Lubavitcher Rebbe
**Source:** Sichos In English, Volume 20

---

The discourse begins by discussing the Jewish practice of greeting one another with blessings when gathering together. As stated, "when two Jews meet, therefore, they greet each other with the blessings, 'Peace unto you' and 'Unto you peace.'"

## The Significance of Nissan

The Rebbe emphasizes that Nissan differs from Tishrei in that it represents miraculous rather than natural conduct. The month's name contains numerical allusions to "miracles within miracles."

## Human Responsibility

The teaching stresses that humans possess tremendous power to influence creation. Individuals should fulfill the command to "Be fruitful and multiply" while educating children in righteous conduct.

## Education and Governance

The discourse advocates for implementing a "moment of silence" in schools to foster recognition of the Creator, arguing this approach avoids sectarian disputes while accomplishing spiritual goals.

## Redemption and Tanya

The text concludes by connecting the printing of 1,000 editions of Tanya (a foundational Chassidic text) with preparing the world for messianic redemption, noting that "Mashiach will come when 'Your wellsprings [of Chassidus] shall spread forth.'"
