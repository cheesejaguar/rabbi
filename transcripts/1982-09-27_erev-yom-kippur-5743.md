# Erev Yom Kippur, 5743

**Date:** September 27, 1982 (Erev Yom Kippur 5743)
**Occasion:** Erev Yom Kippur
**Source:** Sichos In English, Volume 15

---

1. The teachings address Erev Yom Kippur, teshuvah, eating and drinking on this day, and the connection to Parshas Berachah. Material and spiritual blessings are discussed, along with unity among Jews and the promise of redemption through Moshiach.

2. The continuation discusses deeds and their relationship to physical and spiritual matters, connected to the theme of Shabbos.

3. The final section addresses unity as a vessel for blessings, the various religious campaigns and practices, and the promise of redemption.
